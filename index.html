In Agatha Christie news this week:
Helen Byron, historian and tv presenter, will be giving a talk about 'The Murder of Roger Ackroyd' at the Cheltenham Festival next June. Tickets will go on sale in October.
Our annual meet up 'Return to Solidier's Island' returns in July 2023. Since the last three were such a success, we've extended it over three days. Keep an eye on your inbox for further news and updates!
Black Hawk Productions have acquired the rights to film an adaption of 'The Mousetrap', the long running West End play. Filming will commence in March next year and Viola Davis & Jim Broadbent have their names attached to the project.
